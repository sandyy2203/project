Image to Link

Once you upload your image, it can be converted into a link. This means that by clicking on your image, users will be redirected to a specific URL. Below, you'll find the steps to create a link for your uploaded image:

  1. Upload your image using the form above.
  2. Copy the URL of your uploaded image from the server response.
  3. Use the URL to create a clickable link around your image.

This feature can be useful for sharing images on your website, making your content more interactive and engaging. Whether you want to create image galleries, clickable product images, or add a promotional link to your image, this functionality can help you get it done efficiently!
